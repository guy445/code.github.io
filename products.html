Amul Product Portfolio

Amul Gold Milk

Launched: 1996
Price: ₹66/liter
Market Cap: ₹5,200 Cr

Premium full-cream milk with 4.5% fat content, providing rich taste and nutritional benefits.

Core Differentiation

Higher fat content than competitors, sourced directly from farmer cooperatives ensuring freshness and quality.

Distinctiveness

Processed using advanced UHT technology for extended shelf life without preservatives.

Amul Butter

Launched: 1955
Price: ₹54/100g
Market Cap: ₹8,500 Cr

Iconic table butter made from fresh cream with a distinctive taste that has defined generations.

Core Differentiation

Made from pasteurized cream with no added colors or preservatives, creating a natural buttery taste.

Distinctiveness

Recognized by the Amul Girl mascot, one of India's longest-running advertising campaigns since 1966.

Amul Cheese

Launched: 1964
Price: ₹125/200g
Market Cap: ₹4,800 Cr

Processed cheese with a mild flavor, perfect for sandwiches, toast, and cooking applications.

Core Differentiation

Made from natural cheese with no artificial additives, providing authentic dairy flavor.

Distinctiveness

Versatile product available in blocks, slices, spreads, and cubes to suit different consumer needs.

Amul Ice Cream

Launched: 1996
Price: ₹220/liter
Market Cap: ₹3,900 Cr

Real milk ice cream made with fresh cream and natural ingredients in various flavors.

Core Differentiation

Made with real milk fat instead of vegetable oils, creating a creamier texture and richer taste.

Distinctiveness

Offers unique Indian flavors like Kesar Pista and Malai Kulfi alongside international favorites.

Amul Kool

Launched: 2002
Price: ₹25/200ml
Market Cap: ₹1,800 Cr

Flavored milk beverages available in various flavors including chocolate, strawberry, and kesar.

Core Differentiation

Made with real milk and natural flavors, providing nutrition along with refreshment.

Distinctiveness

Offers unique Indian flavors like Kesar and Thandai, catering to local taste preferences.

Amul Dahi (Yogurt)

Launched: 1994
Price: ₹40/400g
Market Cap: ₹2,100 Cr

Traditional Indian yogurt made from pasteurized milk with active cultures for gut health.

Core Differentiation

Contains probiotic cultures that aid digestion and boost immunity, with no added preservatives.

Distinctiveness

Perfect balance of thickness and tanginess that complements Indian cuisine.

Amul Lite Bread Spread

Launched: 2001
Price: ₹50/100g
Market Cap: ₹1,200 Cr

Low-fat butter spread with 40% less fat than regular butter, ideal for health-conscious consumers.

Core Differentiation

Provides the taste of butter with reduced fat content, making it heart-friendly.

Distinctiveness

Maintains spreadability even when refrigerated, unlike regular butter.

Amul Mozzarella Cheese

Launched: 2006
Price: ₹380/1kg
Market Cap: ₹1,500 Cr

Stretchy, mild-flavored cheese perfect for pizzas, pastas, and other Italian dishes.

Core Differentiation

Made with standardized process ensuring consistent melting properties and stretch.

Distinctiveness

Available in both block and shredded formats for different cooking applications.
